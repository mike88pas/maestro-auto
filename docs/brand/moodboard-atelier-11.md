## OpenArt prompts — generacja w 4 batchach

### Batch 1: Logo direction (3 warianty, 4 wariantów każdy = 12 obrazów)

**Setup:** OpenArt → Flux Pro 1.1, aspect 1:1, batch of 4.

#### Logo A — Wordmark (zalecany primary)
```
Architectural luxury wordmark logo: the word "ATELIER" in classical
Playfair Display serif typeface in off-white #FAFAF7, followed by the
numeral "11" in matte champagne gold #B08D57, separated by a fine
vertical hairline 1px register mark, on deep ink-black background #0B0B0F,
framed in vast negative space with subtle 12-column grid lines barely
visible in dark gray, evocative of architectural firm signage like
Foster + Partners or Herzog & de Meuron, fine art quality, museum
poster aesthetic, 4k
```

#### Logo B — Monogram
```
Luxury monogram: capital letter A formed with classical Playfair Display
hairline serif strokes interlocked with two vertical lines representing
numeral 11, the two lines exactly the height of A's apex, color matte
champagne gold #B08D57, centered on ink-black background #0B0B0F,
embossed leather seal style with subtle 3D depth and gold leaf finish,
evocative of a Savile Row tailor's chest stamp, no other elements,
hyper-realistic 4k product photography
```

#### Logo C — Sigil (jedenastnik)
```
Geometric luxury sigil composed of exactly 11 thin vertical lines of
equal weight in matte champagne gold #B08D57, arranged in a perfect
square format on ink-black background #0B0B0F, suggesting both
barcode rhythm and architectural facade fenestration, with one of the
11 lines slightly elongated below baseline as signature mark, evocative
of Japanese family crest mon meets modernist architectural mark,
no text, no embellishment, museum-quality presentation, 4k
```

## Forbidden in generation

- ❌ Lifestyle modeli (osób z twarzami — only hands at work)
- ❌ Sunset / golden hour
- ❌ Drone shots
- ❌ Warm grain / film stock simulation (Atelier 11 = sharp, modern, technical)
- ❌ Wielokolorowy accent (tylko champagne gold)
- ❌ Polski rejestr w widoku
- ❌ Watermarki, logo producenta widoczne
